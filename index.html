Wendi Coffman- Porter

Author of Alternate Reality

I am a storyteller who thrives at the crossroads of science fiction, fantasy, and history. My novels explore the tension between light and shadow, science and magic, love and betrayal—always through the lens of complex characters and immersive worlds. Whether unraveling the mysteries of galactic empires, delving into the grit of espionage, or charting the dangerous paths of forbidden romance, my work invites readers to step into stories that challenge, enthrall, and linger long after the final page. Available in bookstores worldwide.

About the Author

I've been crafting stories since the 1980s, when I was just a kid with a wild imagination and a typewriter. What started as childhood adventures has evolved into a lifelong passion for creating worlds that readers can lose themselves in.

As a traditional author, my books grace the shelves of Barnes & Noble and independent bookstores across the country. There's something magical about walking into a bookstore and seeing your work among the greats—it's a dream I've been living for decades.

My writing spans multiple genres because I believe great stories transcend categories. Whether it's a space opera that explores the human condition, a fantasy epic that questions the nature of power, or a historical novel that brings the past to life, I'm always chasing that perfect moment when words become magic.
