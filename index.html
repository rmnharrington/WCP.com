About the Author

I've been crafting stories since the 1980s, when I was just a kid with a wild imagination and a typewriter. What started as childhood adventures has evolved into a lifelong passion for creating worlds that readers can lose themselves in.

As a traditional author, my books grace the shelves of Barnes & Noble and independent bookstores across the country. There's something magical about walking into a bookstore and seeing your work among the greatsâ€”it's a dream I've been living for decades.
